Frequently Asked Questions

Here are three frequently asked questions (FAQs) along with their answers

What is included in the free trial plan?

The free trial plan allows you to experience our basic digital marketing services for 14 days at no cost. This includes limited social media management, basic analytics reporting, and access to introductory resources to help you understand how we can benefit your brand. No credit card is required to sign up.

How do you measure the success of digital marketing campaigns?

We measure the success of our digital marketing campaigns through a combination of key performance indicators (KPIs) such as website traffic, engagement rates, lead generation, conversion rates, and return on investment (ROI). We provide regular analytics reports to keep you informed about campaign performance and make data-driven adjustments as needed to ensure optimal results.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes! You can easily upgrade your plan at any time. If you find that you need more comprehensive services or support, simply reach out to your account manager, and they will assist you in transitioning to a higher-tier plan that better suits your needs.
